main module¶

Script to determine leader and follower roles.

Takes input from the camera to determine if the current vehicle should lead or follow based on whether or not there exists an ARTag in front of the vehicle.

If an ARTag exists, the vehicle will become a follower vehicle. If an ARTag does not exist, the vehicle will become the leader vehicle.

Authors: Zein Youssef and Steven Dropiewski

main.decide_role(tag_data)¶

Creates a Leader or Follower object.

Parameters:tag_data – Return value of TagRecognition.detect().
Returns:If tag_data is None, the vehicle will become a Leader. Otherwise, it will become a Follower.
Return type:Leader or Follower
